WHO
ARE
WE?

We are a photography collective devoted to the strange, the spectral, and the sublime. Rooted in a deep commitment to artistic exploration, we support photographers who seek to capture the world through an otherworldly lens—whether surreal, uncanny, or quietly mythic. Through exhibitions, professional master classes, and a two-year rural residency in the mist-laced edges of the Scottish Highlands, we provide a space where imagination and craft meet.

Our mission is to cultivate a thriving creative ecosystem for those who defy convention. We embrace the liminal: the spaces between nature and narrative, history and hallucination, memory and myth. Each of our programs is designed to nurture the artistic process from concept to exhibition, with a focus on technical excellence, conceptual depth, and radical experimentation.

We are deeply proud to exhibit the work of our artists and to help them gain meaningful traction in their creative careers. Through curated showcases, collaborative publications, and partnerships with galleries and institutions, we ensure that our photographers' voices reach far beyond the studio.

Our residency offers artists solitude and community in equal measure—nestled in the windswept borders of the Scottish Highlands, our center becomes both a retreat and a workshop, where photographers are encouraged to push boundaries and develop long-term bodies of work. Here, time slows, the landscape speaks, and the unusual finds room to grow.

We believe in mentorship as much as we believe in mystery. Our master classes are led by professionals whose practices reflect a wide range of approaches, from experimental analog processes to digitally sculpted dreamscapes. We welcome emerging voices as well as seasoned artists looking to reimagine their practice in a space dedicated to the eerie, the ethereal, and the unseen.
